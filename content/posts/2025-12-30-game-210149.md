Navigating the vast world of GTA Online can be a daunting task, but knowing how to fast travel can significantly enhance your gaming experience. Officially introduced by Rockstar in 2023, the Downtown Cab Company Taxi service allows players to quickly reach predetermined locations across the map. While this method is straightforward, it does come with limitations, as you can only travel to specific points of interest such as the Airport, Beach, and Paleto Bay.

For those looking for alternative methods, players have discovered clever ways to fast travel without relying solely on taxis. Owning properties like casinos or yachts opens up additional fast travel options, allowing you to teleport between your owned locations. Moreover, using the Kosatka submarine offers a cost-effective way to travel across the sea, making it a favorite among players who prefer a more adventurous approach.
