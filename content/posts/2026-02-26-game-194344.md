Hungry Studio's flagship title, Block Blast!, has made waves in the gaming industry by being named the "Best Puzzle Game" at the Sensor Tower APAC Awards 2025. This accolade is a testament to the game's widespread appeal, having ranked No. 1 in over 40 markets and being available in more than 200 countries. With its innovative "drag-match-disappear" gameplay, Block Blast! has successfully captivated players around the globe, showcasing the importance of user engagement and retention metrics in today's competitive gaming landscape.

In 2025, Hungry Studio's commitment to localized strategies in the APAC region, including high-score challenges in Japan and celebrity collaborations in Korea, has transformed Block Blast! from a global sensation into a beloved local favorite. Jason Wang, VP of Brand Marketing at Hungry Studio, expressed gratitude for the recognition, emphasizing the company's dedication to continuous improvement and player feedback. This award caps off a remarkable year for Hungry Studio, which has also received multiple honors for Block Blast!, solidifying its position in the casual gaming market.

As we look ahead, it will be intriguing to see how Hungry Studio continues to innovate and adapt its offerings to meet the evolving preferences of players worldwide. Will they push the boundaries of gaming further, or will they focus on refining their existing successes?
