Paramount Pictures is set to expand the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise with a new spin-off film, tentatively titled a "Sonic Universe feature," scheduled for release on December 22, 2028. This announcement comes on the heels of the franchise's remarkable success, particularly with Sonic the Hedgehog 3, which has grossed over $1 billion globally, making it the highest-grossing installment to date.

The upcoming spin-off indicates Paramount's commitment to building a broader Sonic universe, following the fourth movie's release on March 19, 2027. With the franchise's popularity soaring, fans are eager to see how this new project will connect with the existing films and characters.
