Get ready for a wild ride as the highly anticipated urban survival game, Good Luck, is set to debut on PC on April 8, 2026. Developed by the independent Turkish studio Happybara Games, this physics-based platformer promises to deliver a unique blend of chaotic gameplay and lighthearted humor. Players will navigate a city filled with absurd traps and unexpected hazards, all while racing to reach a meeting. With both single-player and online co-op modes for up to five players, Good Luck is shaping up to be a must-play for fans of the genre.

What sets Good Luck apart is its unpredictable physics and the absence of checkpoints, making every playthrough a fresh challenge. The game embraces the spirit of rage games, where players must adapt quickly to survive the urban jungle. Whether you're dodging a banana peel or leaping over a trash can, every moment is filled with potential chaos. As the release date approaches, excitement is building around how players will tackle the games unique challenges together.

As we gear up for the launch, one can't help but wonder: will Good Luck redefine the urban survival genre and become a staple in co-op gaming? Stay tuned for more updates!
