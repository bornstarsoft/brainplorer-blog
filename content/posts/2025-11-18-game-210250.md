With just six attempts to guess a five-letter word, each guess is a thrilling combination of logic and luck. Understanding the feedback from your guessesgreen for correct letters in the right spot, yellow for correct letters in the wrong spot, and grey for letters that aren't in the wordcan significantly enhance your chances of success.

For those looking to improve their gameplay, consider focusing on the structure of the word. For instance, knowing that today's Wordle (1612) contains only one vowel can help narrow down your options. Additionally, clues about starting and ending letters can guide your guesses effectively. The thematic hints, like associating the word with tools or actions, can also spark ideas that lead to the solution.
