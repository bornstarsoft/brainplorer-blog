In a world where technology and emotions intertwine, "Save My Scrap" emerges as a compelling visual novel and puzzle game that invites players to delve into the intricate relationships between humans and androids. Set to launch on Steam on April 22, 2026, this game not only offers engaging gameplay but also a heart-wrenching narrative that challenges players to confront the emotional realities of their mechanical companions.

Originally based on the free game "Fix My Junk," developed by Unio Anou, the upgraded version promises a richer experience with new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode. Players will step into the shoes of a mechanic tasked with repairing an old android named Harima, only to discover that their journey goes beyond mere mechanics; its about forging connections and unearthing forgotten memories. With multilingual support and a launch discount, this game is set to captivate a global audience.

As we anticipate the release of "Save My Scrap," one can't help but wonder how this game will resonate with players seeking both entertainment and emotional depth. Will it redefine the way we perceive relationships in the age of AI?
