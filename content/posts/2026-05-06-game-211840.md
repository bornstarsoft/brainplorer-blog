In a recent conversation, Alix Wilton Regan, the actress set to portray Lara Croft in the upcoming Tomb Raider games, emphasized the importance of recognizing video game actors for their contributions. Regan highlighted that the work of these performers is often demanding and filled with emotional depth, deserving of acknowledgment in the broader entertainment landscape. She stated, "Video game actors, they deserve their flowers, because we help pull the audience in to elevate the writing, the animation, the direction, the tone."

Regan also praised the cast of Baldurs Gate 3 for inspiring a new wave of appreciation among video game actors. Their performances have sparked a conversation about the value of voice acting and motion capture in gaming, pushing for recognition that has long been overlooked. This shift is crucial, as it not only honors the hard work of these artists but also enhances the overall gaming experience for players.

As the industry evolves, will we see more awards and recognition for the unsung heroes of video games? The future looks promising, and it's time for the gaming community to celebrate these talented individuals.
