November is shaping up to be an exciting month for Xbox Game Pass subscribers, with Microsoft unveiling a lineup of 11 new titles, including the highly anticipated Call of Duty: Black Ops 7. This addition is sure to thrill gamers, especially those who subscribe to Game Pass Ultimate and PC Game Pass, as they can dive into this blockbuster title starting November 14.

Among the new releases, players can look forward to a diverse selection of games, such as Dead Static Drive and Sniper Elite Resistance, both launching on November 5. Other notable titles include Whiskerwood and Voidtrain, which promise to deliver unique gaming experiences across various platforms, including cloud, console, and PC.

However, it's not all good news, as six games will be leaving the service on November 15, including Blacksmith Master and STALKER 2: Heart of Chernobyl.
