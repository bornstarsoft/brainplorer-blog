AiliA, the newly launched mirror-based visual puzzle game from IceToad Studio LLC, invites players into a captivating world where reality and reflection intertwine. Available now on Steam for $11.99, with a 20% discount during its first two weeks, AiliA promises an engaging experience for puzzle enthusiasts. The games unique mechanics allow players to manipulate mirrors not just as reflections but as interactive spaces that alter the environment, revealing new paths and solutions to over 100 puzzles.

In AiliA, players step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through beautifully crafted stages that blend the real world with a mirrored counterpart. The puzzles are designed to challenge players' spatial reasoning, featuring elements like water reflections and recursive mirror structures. Additionally, the game includes boss encounters that cleverly incorporate these mirror mechanics, ensuring that each challenge feels fresh and engaging.

For those eager to dive into this reflective adventure, a free demo is available on Steam, allowing players to experience the magic of AiliA firsthand. With a special collaboration bundle that includes Moncage, puzzle fans have even more reason to explore this enchanting game. What mysteries will you uncover in the world of AiliA?
