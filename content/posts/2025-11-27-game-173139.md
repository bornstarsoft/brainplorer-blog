At the Golden Joystick Awards 2025, Ghost of Yotei emerged victorious, clinching the coveted title of Console Game of the Year. This action-adventure game, developed by Sucker Punch Productions, serves as a standalone sequel to the acclaimed Ghost of Tsushima, and it has captivated audiences with its stunning visuals and engaging narrative. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of Mount Yotei in Hokkaido, Japan, players step into the shoes of Atsu, a mercenary driven by vengeance, 329 years after the events of its predecessor.

What sets Ghost of Yotei apart is not just its gripping storyline but also its innovative gameplay mechanics. The introduction of a "weapon counter system" differentiates Atsu from Jin Sakai, offering players a fresh combat experience. Coupled with dynamic weather effects that reflect the real climate of Mount Yotei, the game immerses players in a richly detailed world that feels alive.
