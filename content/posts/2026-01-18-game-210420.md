In the vast ocean of free games on Steam, its easy for hidden gems to get overlooked. One such title is "12, Memory Lane," which has recently garnered "Very Positive" reviews since its release on December 15, 2025. This game not only offers an engaging narrative but also showcases the incredible potential of indie developers, making it a must-try for any gaming enthusiast.

Set in the eerie estate of the late Henry Villemaine, players embark on a journey to unravel the mystery behind the Villemaine familys disappearance. With its blend of exploration and puzzle-solving, "12, Memory Lane" invites players to delve into the past through Henrys journals, creating an immersive experience that lasts about an hour and 45 minutes. Despite some critiques regarding its controls and length, the emotional depth of the story resonates with players, proving that great narratives dont always require lengthy gameplay.

As we continue to see innovative titles emerge from indie studios like Forget-Me-Not, its clear that the landscape of gaming is evolving. Will you add "12, Memory Lane" to your library and experience this captivating story for yourself?
