In a bold move to reshape the gaming landscape, Arjan Brussee, co-founder of Guerilla Games and former technical director at Epic Games, is developing 'The Immense Engine'a game engine designed to provide a distinctly European alternative to the dominant American and Chinese technologies like Unreal Engine and Unity. Brussee emphasizes the need for a fully European-hosted engine that adheres to local regulations, aiming to cater not only to gaming but also to sectors such as defense and logistics.

Brussee's vision for The Immense Engine includes innovative construction principles and a deep integration of AI, which he believes will revolutionize how game engines are developed. He critiques existing engines for being outdated, stating they were designed for traditional workflows that rely heavily on mouse interactions. With AI's rise, he sees a unique opportunity to streamline development processes, potentially allowing a small team to accomplish what would typically require many more.

As the gaming industry continues to evolve, the introduction of The Immense Engine could signal a significant shift in how developers approach game creation. Will this new engine pave the way for a more diverse and innovative gaming ecosystem in Europe? Let's keep an eye on this exciting development.
