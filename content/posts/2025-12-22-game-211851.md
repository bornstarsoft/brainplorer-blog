The Octopath Traveler series has reached an impressive milestone, surpassing 6 million units sold across its titles. This achievement, announced by Square Enix, includes sales from the original Octopath Traveler, its sequel, and the newly released Octopath Traveler 0. This marks a significant growth from the 5 million units reported just a year ago, showcasing the series' growing popularity among gamers worldwide.

The first game in the series has been a standout success, with over 3 million units sold by September 2022, while Octopath Traveler II contributed an additional million units by June 2023. With a holiday season discount of 60% on the first two games, now is the perfect time for new players to dive into this captivating RPG universe. As the series continues to evolve, one can't help but wonder what new adventures await in the future for fans of Octopath Traveler.
